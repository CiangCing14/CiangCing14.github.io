Mulhouse | 10.03.2024 | 8 March International Working Women's Day was a wide participation in the march in Mulhouse, France. Organized by the French institutions in the night march organized by the women's platform created in 2019 under the name “Feministte Du 68”; New women, ADKH, Kurdish Women's Movement, Alevi Cultural Center and Didf attended. The march, which passed, ended in Place de La Reunion with slogans, songs and dances in every language.
