We publish the pictures sent to us by our readers. The photos were taken of posters and stickers in the city of Valkekoski, near Tampere. Valkeakoski is an industrial city that has traditionally been strong supporting the labor movement, which is reflected, for example, in the events of the 1918 Finnish Workers' Revolution. At that time, hundreds of the best boys and daughters in the city's working class were murdered by white bays.
