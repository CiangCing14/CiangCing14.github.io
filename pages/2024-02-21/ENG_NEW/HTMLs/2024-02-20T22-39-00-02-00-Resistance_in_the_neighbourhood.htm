Solidarity intervention in the struggle of farmers and students was carried out by the KKE (M-L) on Saturday, February 17th at the Bazaar of Xanthi. With announcements and distribution of notices, he highlighted the right of the struggles, their importance to the whole people, and the need for these struggles to collect the maximum solidarity and support from all the popular strata.
